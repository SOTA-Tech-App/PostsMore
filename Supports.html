Refund Policy

Refunds for purchases made through the App Store are subject to Apple's refund policy. To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Open your iTunes or App Store.
  2. Click on your Apple ID and choose "View Apple ID".
  3. Enter your password.
  4. Click on "Purchase history".
  5. Click on "Report a problem".
  6. Select the purchase you want a refund for and describe the problem.
  7. Submit your request.

Apple will then review your request and contact you via email to let you know if your refund has been approved.

If you have any questions about the refund process, please contact Apple Support.
